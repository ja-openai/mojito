    How to use the controls
    
            
      
              
    • LTR/RTL/Auto textarea changes only the typing field base direction.
      • 
              
    • LTR/RTL/Auto preview changes only the rendered output base direction.
      • 
              
    • LRM/RLM/ALM are point marks. They do not wrap text and do not close.
      • 
              
    • LRI/RLI/FSI ... PDI isolate inserted values. PDI closes any of the three isolate starts.
      • 
            
    
            
    
              
    LRM
    
              
    Left-to-right mark. A small LTR hint at one position, often for punctuation or boundaries.
    
              
    RLM
    
              
    Right-to-left mark. A small RTL hint at one position, often for punctuation or boundaries.
    
              
    ALM
    
              
    Arabic letter mark. Like an RTL hint with Arabic-specific behavior around numbers and punctuation.
    
              
    LRI ... PDI
    
              
    Wrap a known LTR span, such as ChatGPT, OpenAI, a URL, code, or an ID inside RTL text.
    
              
    RLI ... PDI
    
              
    Wrap a known RTL span, such as a Hebrew or Arabic name inside LTR text.
    
              
    FSI ... PDI
    
              
    First Strong Isolate. The F means first strong character; the span direction is inferred from the first strong letter inside. Use it for unknown user text, not for known LTR brands/code.
